MY STORY

Behind the Lens

The journey, philosophy, and passion that drive my photography

EST. 2014

Finding Light in Every Moment

My journey into photography began over a decade ago, when I first picked up a camera to document the stunning landscapes of my homeland, Georgia. What started as a personal passion quickly evolved into a lifelong dedication to the art of visual storytelling.

After studying fine art photography in Tbilisi and completing workshops in Europe, I returned to my hometown of Kutaisi to establish my studio. Here, surrounded by ancient architecture and breathtaking nature, I found my true voice as a photographer.

Today, I specialize in creating authentic, emotionally resonant imagery that captures the essence of my subjects. Whether it's the intimacy of a wedding, the power of a portrait, or the energy of a commercial campaign, my approach remains the same — to reveal the beauty that already exists.

— Anuka Abazadze
