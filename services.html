FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with me

How far in advance should I book? +
For portrait sessions, 2-4 weeks in advance is recommended. For weddings, 6-12 months is ideal, especially during peak season (May-October).
Do you travel outside of Kutaisi? +
Yes! I photograph throughout Georgia and internationally. Travel fees may apply depending on the location. Contact me for a custom quote.
How will I receive my photos? +
You'll receive a private online gallery with high-resolution downloads, sharing options, and professional print ordering. Delivery time is 2-4 weeks for portraits, 4-6 weeks for weddings.
Can I order prints and albums? +
Absolutely! I offer professional fine art prints, canvases, and premium heirloom albums through my lab partners. All products are archival quality.
What's your cancellation policy? +
A 50% non-refundable retainer is required to secure your date. The remaining balance is due one week before the session. In case of emergency, we can reschedule subject to availability.
